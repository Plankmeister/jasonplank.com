01 About Me [JP/01]

Connecting
the Dots

Most law firms are sitting on untapped digital potential — I've spent over two decades helping unlock it. As the Digital Marketing Manager at Baker Donelson, one of the nation's largest law firms, I lead the firm's digital presence across web, email, SEO, social media, data analytics, and podcast production.

My work sits at the intersection of technology, creativity, and business development — translating complex platforms into practical tools that help attorneys build client relationships and grow revenue. That means optimizing websites for visibility, building email campaigns that drive measurable engagement, and training attorneys to use social media with confidence.

Lately, I've been deeply focused on how AI can accelerate and elevate this work, from streamlining content production to surfacing insights in data faster than ever before. I'm at my best in complex environments where connecting the dots between technology and business strategy actually matters.
